The implementation of the Goods and Services Tax (GST) in India has significantly transformed how businesses manage taxes. Launched in 2017, GST streamlined the indirect tax system, reducing the complexities of various tax types like VAT, service tax, and excise duty.

While beneficial for the overall business ecosystem, managing GST in an e-commerce store, especially for a multi-vendor marketplace, can be challenging. With multiple vendors, varying GST rates, and different compliance needs, automating the GST process is essential for efficiency and accuracy.

For Magento 2 store owners running multi-vendor setups, several extensions are available to simplify the task of automating GST calculations. These extensions ensure compliance with Indian GST regulations, making tax management hassle-free for both administrators and vendors.

In this blog, we’ll explore the best Magento 2 multi-vendor GST extensions that cater to Indian businesses, helping you choose the right one for your store.
