Best Magento 2 SMTP Extensions | Free & Premium

Published on: August 12, 2024

In today’s digital age, effective email communication is crucial for any online business. For Magento 2 store owners, ensuring that emails reach customers’ inboxes rather than spam folders is essential for successful marketing campaigns and customer engagement. This is where SMTP (Simple Mail Transfer Protocol) extensions come into play. These extensions enhance email deliverability by allowing store owners to configure reliable SMTP servers. In this blog, we’ll explore some of the best Magento 2 SMTP extensions available, focusing on their features, benefits, and how they can improve your store’s email communication.

1. Magento 2 SMTP by Meetanshi

The Magento 2 SMTP by Meetanshi extension is a powerful tool designed to enhance email deliverability for Magento stores. With this extension, you can send emails via popular services like Mandrill, Amazon AWS, or Gmail, significantly reducing the chances of your emails ending up in spam folders. This ensures that your email campaigns reach your customers effectively.

Key Features:

  • Multiple Email Providers: Send emails through Mandrill, Amazon AWS, or Gmail.
  • Email Tracking: A backend grid to track all sent emails, providing insights into your email campaigns.
  • Compatibility: Supports Magento CE, EE 2.3.x, 2.4.x.
  • Professional Support: While the extension is free, extended support services are available for professional assistance.

User Rating: 100% based on 3 reviews.This extension is ideal for businesses looking to enhance their email marketing efforts with reliable delivery and tracking capabilities.

2. Webkul Magento 2 SMTP Extension

The Webkul Magento 2 SMTP Extension allows store owners to easily set up an SMTP server on their web store. This extension supports various host providers, enabling flexible email sending options.

Key Features:

  • Simple Configuration: Easily set up an SMTP host for your store.
  • Error Notifications: If emails fail to deliver, error messages are displayed on the SMTP settings page.
  • Test Email Functionality: Store owners can test the extension by sending test emails to ensure proper configuration.
  • Multiple Host Providers: Choose from providers like Zimbra, Gmail, Amazon SES, and Hotmail.

This extension is perfect for store owners who want a straightforward setup and reliable email delivery without the hassle of complex configurations.

3. FME Magento 2 SMTP Extension

The FME Magento 2 SMTP Extension offers customizable email sending settings, allowing store owners to enhance their email communication effectively. This extension helps reduce the risk of emails being marked as spam.

Key Features:

  • Custom SMTP Configuration: Customize SMTP settings to suit your store’s needs.
  • Email Log System: View sent emails with status updates and automatically clear email logs.
  • Testing Capabilities: Test your SMTP configuration to ensure successful email delivery.

This extension is ideal for businesses that require advanced customization and logging features to monitor their email campaigns.

4. Amasty SMTP Email Settings for Magento 2

The Amasty SMTP Email Settings for Magento 2 extension ensures reliable email delivery through custom SMTP configuration. It supports secure connections with trusted email providers, enhancing your store’s email reputation.

Key Features:

  • Secure Mail Transfer: Choose from predefined providers or set up your own secure SMTP configuration.
  • OAuth 2.0 Support: Supports Microsoft OAuth 2.0 for secure authentication with Office365.
  • Debug Mode: Emulates SMTP server operations without sending emails, allowing you to identify and fix errors.

This extension is a great choice for businesses looking to improve their email marketing performance by ensuring secure and reliable email delivery.

5. Plumrocket Magento 2 SMTP Extension

The Plumrocket Magento 2 SMTP Extension offers comprehensive SMTP settings, including host, port, encryption, and authentication options. It supports over 40 SMTP providers, making it a versatile choice for store owners.

Key Features:

  • Wide Provider Support: Choose from a wide range of SMTP providers.
  • Test Email Feature: Test new SMTP settings before making them live.
  • Open Code: 100% open code for easy customization and integration.

This extension is suitable for businesses that require flexibility and a wide range of provider options for their email communication needs.

Conclusion

Choosing the right SMTP extension for your Magento 2 store can significantly impact your email marketing success. These extensions improve email deliverability and provide valuable features like tracking, testing, and customization. Whether you’re looking for a simple setup or advanced customization options, these extensions offer solutions to enhance your store’s email communication. By integrating a reliable SMTP extension, you can ensure that your emails reach your customers’ inboxes, boosting engagement and driving sales.
