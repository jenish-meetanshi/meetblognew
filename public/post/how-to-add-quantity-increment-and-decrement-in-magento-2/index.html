How to Add Quantity Increment and Decrement in Magento 2

Published on: February 14, 2023

Magento 2 offers a robust platform for building an e-commerce store. One of the critical features of an e-commerce store is the ability to adjust the quantity of products in the cart. In this blog post, we will walk you through the process of adding quantity increment and decrement functionality in Magento 2. It’s worth noting that implementing this feature can significantly enhance the shopping experience for your customers by making it easier for them to modify product quantities in their cart without having to remove and re-add items.

Moreover, a user-friendly quantity selection process can encourage customers to make larger purchases and decrease the number of abandoned carts. The appearance of the quantity box on the product page and compatibility with various devices are also crucial for providing a smooth shopping experience for customers. In this step-by-step guide, we will explain how to add quantity increment and decrement in Magento 2.

Here are some additional points that you can include in your blog:

Customizing the Increment and Decrement Buttons: 

In Magento 2, you can also customize the appearance of the increment and decrement buttons. You can do this by editing the CSS files associated with the buttons or by using a frontend development tool such as LESS or Sass.

Increment and Decrement for Configurable Products: 

If you have configurable products in your store or else you can also create configurable product in Magento 2, You may want to set different increment and decrement values for each variant of the product. In this case, you can create custom options for each variant and set the desired increment and decrement value for each option.

Importance of User-Friendly Quantity Selection: 

A user-friendly quantity selection process can greatly enhance the shopping experience for your customers. By making it easy for customers to change the quantity of products in their cart, you can encourage them to make larger purchases and reduce the number of abandoned carts.

Product Page Display: 

The appearance of the quantity box on the product page can also impact the shopping experience for your customers. Consider using a drop-down or text box that is easily noticeable and allows customers to quickly change the quantity of a product.

Compatibility with Different Devices: 

It’s also important to ensure that the quantity increment and decrement buttons are compatible with different devices, including desktop computers, laptops, tablets, and smartphones. This will ensure that customers can change the quantity of products in their cart regardless of the device they are using.

Steps to Add Quantity Increment and Decrement in Magento 2

Step 1: Log in to your Magento 2 admin panel

Go to your Magento 2 website’s admin panel and log in with your credentials.

Step 2: Navigate to Store Configuration

Once logged in, navigate to “Stores” -> “Configuration.” This will take you to the store configuration section where you can change various settings for your store.

Step 3: Select “Product Details”

Under “Catalog,” select “Product Details.” This section contains all the settings related to product details and display.

Step 4: Scroll down to “Qty Increments”

Scroll down to the “Qty Increments” section. This section allows you to change the increment and decrement step for product quantities in the cart.

Step 5: Enter the desired value for “Qty Increments”

Enter the desired value for “Qty Increments.” This value represents the step that the quantity will increase or decrease when you click the increment or decrement button. For example, if you enter “2,” the quantity will increase by 2 with each click of the increment button.

Step 6: Save the configuration

Save the configuration by clicking on “Save Config.” This will save your changes and apply them to your store.

Conclusion 

That concludes the process! You have successfully added the quantity increment and decrement functionality to Magento 2. To verify this, simply navigate to a product page and inspect the quantity box. You should now see buttons to increment and decrement the quantity.

In summary, integrating quantity increment and decrement functionality in Magento 2 is a straightforward process that can be accomplished in just a few steps. This feature is vital for enhancing the shopping experience for your customers and simplifying the process of adjusting the quantity of products in their cart.
