Complete Guide: How to Attach PDF to a Product in Magento 2

Published on: February 14, 2023

Magento 2 is a widely popular e-commerce platform that provides businesses with a range of powerful tools to create and manage an online store. Its advanced features and customization options allow businesses to improve the customer shopping experience and increase sales.

One of the key features of Magento 2 is the ability to attach PDF files to products. This feature allows customers to access important information such as product manuals, warranties, technical specifications, usage instructions, and more. Providing customers with this information can help them make informed purchasing decisions, resulting in a positive shopping experience and increased customer satisfaction.

In this blog, we will provide a detailed guide on how to attach PDF files to products in Magento 2. This step-by-step guide is intended for how to attach PDF to products in Magento 2 store for the store administrators and merchants who want to enhance their product listings and provide their customers with valuable information.

Steps to attach PDF to a product in Magento 2

Step 1: Create a PDF File

The first step to attaching a PDF file to a product in Magento 2 is to create the PDF file itself. You can create a PDF file using any number of tools such as Microsoft Word, Adobe Acrobat, or even a free online PDF converter. The key is to make sure that the PDF file contains all of the relevant information that you want to provide to your customers.

Step 2: Upload the PDF File to Magento 2

Once you have created your PDF file, you will need to upload it to your Magento 2 installation. To do this, log in to the Magento 2 backend and navigate to the “Content” section. From there, click on the “Media” option and then select “Files.” From here, you can drag and drop your PDF file into the upload box, or you can click on the “Choose File” button to select the file from your computer.

Step 3: Attach the PDF File to a Product

With the PDF file uploaded to Magento 2, you can now attach it to a product. To do this, log in to the Magento 2 backend and navigate to the “Products” section. From there, select the product that you want to attach the PDF file to and then click on the “Edit” button.

Once you are in the product edit screen, scroll down to the “Downloadable Information” section. From here, you can click on the “Add Link” button to add the PDF file to the product. When you do this, you will be prompted to provide a title for the link, as well as to select the PDF file that you uploaded in step 2.

Step 4: Save the Product

With the PDF file attached to the product, you can now save your changes. To do this, simply scroll to the top of the screen and click on the “Save” button. You can then navigate to the product page on your Magento 2 store to confirm that the PDF file is now attached to the product.

Conclusion

Attaching a PDF file to a product in Magento 2 is a simple and effective way to improve the customer shopping experience. It allows businesses to provide customers with vital product information, such as warranties, manuals, and technical specifications.
