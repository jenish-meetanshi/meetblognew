How to Configure Magento 2 Price Scope

Published on: April 17, 2023

Magento 2 is an e-commerce platform renowned for its versatility and robustness, offering users an array of features and functionalities. Pricing is a vital element of any online store, and Magento 2 provides an all-encompassing pricing system that empowers store owners to regulate their product prices, considering factors like customer groups, websites, and currencies.

The price scope is a crucial configuration setting within Magento 2’s pricing system. In this blog post, we will explore the process of how to configure Magento 2 price scope.

What is the Price Scope in Magento 2?

Price scope in Magento 2 determines the level at which prices are defined and managed. Magento 2 offers two price scopes:

Global scope: Prices are set globally and apply to all websites and stores within a Magento instance.

Website scope: Prices are set at the website level, which means that prices can differ between different websites within a Magento instance.

When creating a new store in Magento 2, you have the option to choose the price scope. You can also change the price scope later from the Magento admin panel.

How to Configure Price Scope in Magento 2

Configuring the price scope in Magento 2 involves a few simple steps. Let’s take a look at them:

Step 1: Log in to the Magento Admin Panel

Log in to your Magento 2 admin panel using your credentials.

Step 2: Navigate to Stores > Settings > Configuration

In the admin panel, navigate to Stores > Settings > Configuration.

Step 3: Select Catalog from the left-hand menu

In the Configuration page, select the Catalog option from the left-hand menu.

Step 4: Expand the Price section

In the Catalog section, expand the Price section by clicking on it.

Step 5: Choose the Price Scope

Under the Price section, you will find the Price Scope option. You can choose either the Global or Website scope from the dropdown menu.

Step 6: Save the Configuration

After selecting the Price Scope, click on the Save Config button to save the changes.

Congratulations! You have successfully configured the price scope in Magento 2.

Conclusion

Setting up the price scope correctly in Magento 2 is a vital component of managing your product pricing effectively. With the right price scope and use custom price templates, you can establish prices that are tailored and accurate to your customers’ needs, resulting in an improved shopping experience.

It’s essential to bear in mind that once you’ve set the price scope, changing it to the other scope necessitates resetting your store completely. Therefore, it’s imperative to select the right price scope for your store at the outset.
