How to Customize Magento 2 Email Templates?

Published on: May 15, 2023

If you’re looking to learn how to customize Magento 2 email templates, you’ve come to the right place. In Magento 2, transactional emails serve as the main communication method to inform customers about their order status, shipping details, refunds, and other pertinent information related to their accounts. With Magento, you can customize several pre-made email templates and include personalized variables to make them unique.

It’s essential to tailor these email templates to align with your business objectives. To help with this, this guide will teach you how to customize Magento 2 email templates.

You can watch a brief video on managing email templates in Magento 2, or follow the instructions provided below.

Detailed Guide for Customize Email Template in Magento 2

1. Load the template: Access the transactional emails in Magento 2 by navigating to Admin Panel > Marketing > Communications > Email Templates. From there, you can either choose an email template you want to edit or add a new one by clicking the “Add New Template” button. If adding a new template, select the email template you want to base it on in the “Template” field and click the “Load Template” button.

2. Fill in the template information: Set the name of the template in the “Template Name” field, then modify the “Template Subject” and “Content” as desired. Adding “Template Styles” is optional. Once you’ve finished with the configurations, click the “Save Template” button.

3. Assign the email template: If you’ve created a new template, you need to assign it in the store configurations. For instance, if you created a new order template, go to Admin Panel > Stores > Settings > Configuration > Sales > Sales Emails (note that the path may differ depending on the template). Find the template select box, uncheck “Use system value,” and choose the template from the list. Once all configurations are set, click the “Save Config” button and flush the Magento caches.

4. Customize the email logo: After creating and assigning the email template to the store configurations, you need to customize the email logo. To do so, navigate to Content > Design > Configuration and select the configuration scope you want to customize the email logo for. Move to the “Transactional Emails” section and fill out the following fields:

  •  Upload the logo image or select from the gallery of previously uploaded images.
  •  Set the logo image alt.
  •  Enter the logo width and height (note that you must set these characteristics if you uploaded the logo instead of selecting it from the gallery).
  •  Choose the header and footer templates.

When you’re done, don’t forget to save the configuration.

To customize email templates in Magento 2, follow these steps:

1. Go to Marketing > Communications > Email Templates to edit or create a new email template.

2. Go to Stores > Settings > Configuration > Sales > Sales Emails and select the template you want to modify in the template select box.

3. Go to Content > Design > Configuration to customize the email logo.
