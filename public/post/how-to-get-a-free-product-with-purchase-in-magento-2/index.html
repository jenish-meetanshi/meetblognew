How to get free product-with-purchase for your Magento 2 store?

Step 1: Create a new Cart Price Rule for the promotion

Firstly, log in to your Admin Account and select Marketing > Promotions > Cart Price Rule from the left sidebar.

Next, click the “Add New Rule” button to start setting up the new rule for the gift-with-purchase promotion.

This will take you to the “New Cart Price Rule” page where you need to complete the settings of 4 tabs:

  •  Rule information
  •  Conditions
  •  Actions
  •  Labels

Fill in the required fields for the “Rule Information” tab, just like you would when creating any new Cart Price Rule.

If you are applying several cart price rules simultaneously, it’s recommended to set the activation time and rule priority for the new rule to manage your rules better.

Step 2: Set up Cart Price Conditions on the Condition Tab

On the Condition tab, retailers need to configure the conditions to trigger the rules. This means that the gift-with-purchase offer on the website is only available when all the conditions are met.

Like other cart price rules, you can specify the scope of the new Cart Price Rule using four options:

  • Product attribute combination
  • Product sub-selection
  • Cart Attribute
  • Condition combination

You can add as many cart price rule conditions as needed by clicking on the (+) icon. To remove any condition, click on the (x) icon.

Step 3: Configure the Cart Price Actions on the Actions tab

Once you have established the conditions for the gift-with-purchase promotion, the next step is to configure the actions on the Actions tab. These actions will be applied to customers’ orders on the storefront when all the specified conditions are met.

First, select Apply = Buy X get Y free (discount amount is Y). This will allow customers to receive a free item when they purchase another item. For example, if they purchase X number of items, they will receive a quantity of Y for free.

Next, fill in the following details for your gift-with-purchase promotion:

  •  Discount Amount: The number of free products customers will receive for their purchase
  •  Maximum Qty Discount is Applied To: The maximum number of times customers can apply this discount
  •  Discount Qty Step (Buy X): The number of items customers must purchase to receive the free product
  •  Apply to Shipping Amount = Yes: The promotion will be applied to the subtotal and shipping amount separately
  •  Discard subsequent rules: Decide whether to restrict the application of other existing discounts with this new cart price rule

Another aspect that can be taken into consideration is the inclusion of an additional condition related to Free Shipping when implementing the “How to Get Free Product With Purchase of Another Product in Magento 2” feature. This condition allows for more flexibility in the gift-with-purchase campaign.

If the Free Shipping option is set to “No,” then free shipping will not be applicable to items included in the gift-with-purchase promotion.

If the Free Shipping option is set to “For matching items only,” then free shipping will be available exclusively for items that fulfill all the conditions outlined in the rule.

On the other hand, if the Free Shipping option is set to “For shipment with matching items,” then free shipping will be provided for shipments that include the qualifying items.

By incorporating this additional condition, merchants can tailor their gift-with-purchase campaigns to align with their specific shipping policies and customer preferences.

For example, if you want to offer a “Buy 2 Get 1 Free” promotion on your website, with no maximum number of free items gifted, the cart price rule would be set up as follows:

  •  Apply = Buy X get Y free (discount amount is Y)
  •  Discount amount = 1
  •  Maximum Quantity Discount is Applied = 0
  •  Discount Qty Step (Buy X) = 2

Check out more examples of different gift-with-purchase offers for your e-store on the backend.

Step 4: Set the conditions for the products applied to the new Cart Price Rule

Towards the end of the Actions tab, you will find the option to configure the settings for this action. Retailers have the flexibility to define rules for particular items on their websites, enabling them to provide a “free gift-with-purchase” promotion according to their desired criteria.

Step 5: Set the Cart Price Labels in the Labels section

To assign the rule label for all store views, simply enter the desired text within the designated area provided for the “Default Rule Label for All Store Views” section. In case your online store has multiple store views, you can also customize and set specific labels for each of them by utilizing the dedicated “Store View Specific Labels” section. This allows you to tailor the rule labels according to the unique characteristics of each store view.

Step 6: Save and apply the new Rule

Once you have finished following the aforementioned steps, proceed by clicking the “Save Rule” button. This action will effectively save and activate the rule on your e-commerce website, ensuring its implementation and functionality as intended.
