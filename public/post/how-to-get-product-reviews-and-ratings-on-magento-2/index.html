Why are product reviews important for any eCommerce business?

Around 88% of potential customers consider online reviews to be as reliable as personal recommendations from friends. It makes them beneficial for online stores for the following reasons:

1. They provide valuable information to potential customers: Reviews give customers an idea of what to expect from a product, including its quality, features, and performance. This information can help customers make informed purchasing decisions.

2. They increase trust and credibility: Positive reviews can increase trust and credibility with potential customers, making them more likely to make a purchase.

3. They increase conversions: As shown by various studies, products with reviews have a higher conversion rate than those without reviews. Positive reviews can lead to increased sales.

4. They help with SEO: Having reviews on your product pages can help improve your website’s search engine optimization (SEO) by providing additional content for search engines to crawl.

5. They can help identify areas for improvement: Negative reviews can help businesses identify areas where their products or services need improvement.

6. They help customers make a decision: Reviews help customers to understand the pros and cons of a product, which can help them decide whether a product is a good fit for them or not.

How to deal with bad reviews?

Online reviews play a critical role in the purchasing decisions of online shoppers, with a 2021 report by PowerReviews revealing that 99.9% of consumers read reviews before making a purchase. However, not all reviews are created equal.

Positive reviews can increase trust and credibility with potential customers, leading to higher conversions, but receiving 5-star reviews can be challenging. On the other hand, negative reviews are often more common for several reasons:

  •  Emotion: Unsatisfied customers are likelier to be emotional about their experience and share negative feedback. Satisfied customers may not feel as strongly motivated to leave a review.
  •  Sense of injustice: Unsatisfied customers may feel treated unfairly or that their expectations were not met, leading them to leave negative reviews.
  •  Need to vent: Negative reviews may be an outlet for unsatisfied customers to vent their frustrations.
  •  Need to warn others: Unsatisfied customers may leave a negative review to warn others about their experience.
  •  Effort: Writing a review requires effort, and satisfied customers may not feel motivated to take the time to write a positive review.

Acquiring genuine product reviews and rating is a crucial aspect of e-commerce platforms, including Magento 2. While negative reviews present challenges, they can provide potential customers with a realistic understanding of the product and its limitations. In fact, negative reviews offer businesses an opportunity to demonstrate their professionalism by taking responsibility, responding appropriately, offering viable solutions, and maintaining a positive attitude. Effectively handling bad reviews can ultimately transform them into positive ones, contributing to the overall reputation and success of the business. How to add, delete, and manage reviews in Magento 2?

Let’s see how to add, delete, and manage reviews using Magento 2’s built-in functionality.

1. Adding a new review

To enable product reviews for your Magento 2 store, navigate to Stores > Settings > Configuration and select the Catalog option > Product Reviews under the Catalog tab. Set the Enabled field to Yes and Allow Guests to Write Reviews field to Yes so that customers can leave their reviews on the products they’ve purchased. To make a new review visible on the store, go to Marketing > Pending Reviews from the Magento 2 admin, select the review, change its status to Approved, and save changes.

2. Deleting a review

Deleting reviews is generally not recommended as it can negatively affect your business and customer relationships. However, if you decide to delete a review, you can do it using Magento’s native functionality. To delete a review, go to Marketing > All Reviews from the Magento 2 admin, select the review, change its status to Not Approved, or click the Delete Review button and save changes.

3. Managing reviews

You can edit reviews in several ways, such as changing visibility, editing text, or nicknames. To do this, select the review from the All Reviews section and click edit. Alternatively, you can navigate to Catalog > Products, select a product and edit the review under the Product Reviews tab. To manage reviews, click Select > Edit. Once you have made all the necessary edits, save the configurations.
