Steps to integrate ABN AMRO payment to Magento 2:

Step 1: Sign up for an ABN AMRO account

To incorporate ABN AMRO payment into your Magento 2 store, it is necessary to have an account with ABN AMRO. To create an account, you can visit their official website and complete the registration form provided.

Step 2: Install the ABN AMRO Payment Module

After successfully setting up an ABN AMRO account, the next step is to install the ABN AMRO Payment Module on your Magento 2 store. You can acquire the module either from the official ABN AMRO website or through the Magento Marketplace.

Step 3: Configure the ABN AMRO Payment Module

Once you have successfully installed the ABN AMRO Payment Module, the next step is to configure it with your ABN AMRO account information. To do this, navigate to the Magento 2 admin panel and select Stores > Configuration > Sales > Payment Methods > ABN AMRO.

In the configuration panel, you will need to provide your ABN AMRO merchant ID, merchant secret key, and merchant key. You can easily locate these details in your ABN AMRO account dashboard.

Step 4: Test the ABN AMRO Payment Module

Prior to accepting payments through ABN AMRO, it is essential to perform a thorough test of the payment module to ensure its proper functionality. You can accomplish this by placing a test order on your Magento 2 store and selecting ABN AMRO as the payment method.

Upon placing the order, you will be redirected to the ABN AMRO payment page, where you can input your test payment details. After successfully completing the payment, you will be redirected back to your Magento 2 store. This test procedure ensures that the integration of ABN AMRO payment functions smoothly within your store.

Step 5: Go Live with ABN AMRO Payment

After thoroughly testing the ABN AMRO Payment Module and confirming its proper functionality, you can proceed to enable live payments on your Magento 2 store. To do this, simply switch the payment mode from “test” to “live” within the Magento 2 admin panel. This action will enable customers to make actual payments through ABN AMRO on your live store.
