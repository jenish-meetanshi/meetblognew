How to Integrate Barclaycard Payments into Magento 2: A Step-by-Step Guide

Integrating Barclaycard Payments into Magento 2 is a straightforward process that does not necessitate extensive coding knowledge. You can complete the integration by following two main steps: installing the Barclaycard module via composer and configuring the necessary credentials and settings within the backend.

If you’re wondering how to integrate Barclaycard Payments on Magento 2, rest assured that the process is user-friendly and does not require advanced technical expertise. By following the steps below, you’ll be able to seamlessly incorporate Barclaycard Payments into your Magento 2 platform:

Step 1: Install the Barclaycard Module via the Composer

To install the Barclaycard module, merchants need to use the composer. The following steps outline how to install the module:

  • Open the command line interface on your server.
  • Navigate to your Magento 2 root directory.
  • Run the following command:
  • composer requires Barclays/payment

After the module is installed, run the following command:

php bin/magento setup: upgrade

Step 2: Configure the Credentials and Settings in the Backend

After installing the Barclaycard module, merchants can proceed to configure the credentials and settings in the Magento 2 backend. The following steps outline how to configure the module:

  • Log in to your Magento 2 backend.
  • Navigate to Stores > Configuration > Sales > Payment Methods.
  • Under Payment Methods, locate the Barclaycard section and click on it to expand the options.
  • Fill in the credentials required to connect to Barclaycard.

Configure the settings to define how the payment is processed in your store. All configurations are well-instructed in the module user guide.

Protect customers with CVC and 3D secure

Ensuring the security of customers’ card information is crucial for any store using Barclaycard as a payment gateway. Fortunately, Barclaycard offers several layers of security to protect sensitive information, including:

  •  CVC number confirmation
  •  3D secure
  •  SHA-IN and SHA-OUT passphrases

These security measures guarantee that whether customers process payments on the store’s site or on the Barclaycard site, their card information remains safe and secure.

Highlight functions of the Barclaycard module

Most popular cart types are supported:

The Barclaycard payment provides customers with the convenience of checking out using a wide range of popular card types, including Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Maestro, Dinners Club, and JBC. Additionally, the backend empowers store administrators to customize the enabled card types for payment through Barclaycard. This valuable feature ensures flexibility for both customers and Magento stores, as they can select the payment methods that align best with their individual requirements and preferences.

Two ways integration:

The Barclaycard module offers two integration methods for Magento 2 stores: Redirect payment and Direct checkout.

With the Direct payment method, customers can conveniently process their payments directly on your store’s checkout page. The checkout form includes a credit card section integrated among other relevant details. Customers simply need to input their card information, place the order, and the payment will be automatically processed. This method ensures a seamless experience as customers can complete their transactions without leaving your store’s site.

Alternatively, the Redirect payment method directs customers to Barclaycard’s secure hosted payment page to complete the transaction. This integration method prioritizes the safety of shoppers, as all credit card details are processed on Barclaycard’s trusted platform.

Allow creating backend payment:

The saved card information feature offered by the Barclaycard module allows store administrators to assist customers in creating backend orders and facilitating instant payment processing. This convenient functionality not only saves time and effort for both parties but also ensures the accuracy of orders. Customers greatly benefit from this feature, especially when placing orders through email or phone calls, as it streamlines the ordering process and enhances convenience.

Highly secure payment with CVV, 3D, and Request parameters:

The Barclaycard module ensures secure payment processing through three layers of protection:

  •  CVV Number: Customers are required to provide the CVV number on the back of their card to confirm the card’s authority.
  •  3D Secure: Customers must verify their identity by entering their PIN number or banking OTP password.
  •  SHA-IN and SHA-OUT: These values serve as authentication measures for customers’ payment transactions, adding an extra layer of security.
