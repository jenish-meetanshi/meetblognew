What is Freshsales?

Freshsales, a sales CRM platform provided by Freshworks, offers a range of benefits when it comes to streamlining processes, accelerating deal closures, and gaining valuable customer behavior insights. This platform serves businesses across various industry verticals and provides an effective solution for managing customer interactions, both new and existing.

It’s important to highlight that Freshsales is specifically designed to cater to the needs of sales teams. As part of Freshworks’ suite of CRM offerings, it complements other software such as Freshdesk for customer engagement, Freshmarketer for marketing, and Freshservice for IP support.

Thanks to its intuitive and customizable interface, Freshsales seamlessly integrates with your Magento 2 store. This integration is particularly advantageous for individuals who are new to CRM software, as it requires minimal team training for setup.

To learn how to integrate Freshsales with Magento 2 and leverage the benefits of this powerful combination, follow the guidelines below and explore the steps for a successful Freshsales Magento 2 integration.

Integrate Freshsales with Magento 2 via an extension

Integrating Freshsales with your Magento 2 store can be done quickly and easily with the help of an extension. Mageplaza offers the Magento 2 Freshsales integration extension, which is both user-friendly and highly effective.

To set up the integration, you only need to follow two simple steps:

Get your account API

  • Visit this link to create a Freshsales account.
  • After completing your information, click Sign up for free.
  • To get your account API, click your profile picture and choose Settings > API Settings.
  • Copy the API Key so that you can paste it into Magento later.
