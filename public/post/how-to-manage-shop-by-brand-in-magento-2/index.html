How to Manage Shop by Brand in Magento 2

Published on: February 14, 2023

Online store owners widely use Magento 2 as an e-commerce platform to efficiently manage their businesses. It offers a range of features and functionalities that enhance the online shopping experience for customers. Among its features is the “Shop by Brand” option, which helps online stores categorize their products by brand.

This blog post provides a detailed guide on how to manage the “Shop by Brand” feature in Magento 2. It covers various aspects such as creating brand categories, assigning products to specific brands, and customizing brand pages’ display. This comprehensive guide aims on How to Manage Shop by Brand in Magento 2, thereby enhancing their customers’ shopping experience.

Steps to Manage Shop by Brand in Magneto 2

Step 1: Install the Brand Extension

To manage your store by brand in Magento 2, you will first need to install a brand extension. There are several extensions available in the Magento Marketplace, including Amasty’s Shop by Brand and Aheadworks’ Shop by Brand.

Step 2: Create a Brand Attribute

Once you have installed the brand extension, you will need to create a brand attribute. This attribute will be used to categorize your products by brand. To create a brand attribute, navigate to Stores > Attributes > Product and click on the Add New Attribute button. Fill in the necessary information, such as the attribute code and label, and select the Catalog Input Type for Store Owner as Dropdown.

Step 3: Assign Brands to Products

After you have created the brand attribute, you will need to assign brands to your products. To do this, navigate to Catalog > Products and select the product you wish to assign a brand to. From there, scroll down to the Brand Attribute section and select the brand from the dropdown menu. Repeat this step for each product you wish to assign a brand to.

Step 4: Create a Brand Page

Now that you have assigned brands to your products, you will need to create a brand page. This page will display all of the products that belong to a specific brand. To create a brand page, navigate to Content > Pages and click on the Add New Page button. Fill in the necessary information, such as the page title and URL key, and select the brand template from the Layout Update XML section.

Step 5: Add the Brand Navigation Block

Finally, you will need to add the brand navigation block to your store’s menu. This will allow customers to easily navigate to the brand page they are interested in. To add the brand navigation block, navigate to Content > Blocks and click on the Add New Block button. Fill in the necessary information, such as the block title and identifier, and add the following code to the Content section:

php

<ul>

  <li><a href="{{store url='brand/nike'}}">Nike</a></li>

  <li><a href="{{store url='brand/adidas'}}">Adidas</a></li>

  <li><a href="{{store url='brand/puma'}}">Puma</a></li>

</ul>

Replace nike, adidas, and puma with the brands you have assigned to your products.

Conclusion

To sum up, utilizing the “Shop by Brand” feature in Magento 2 can prove to be a valuable tool for product organization and enhancing the customer experience. Creating separate pages for each brand enables customers to conveniently browse and compare products from their preferred brands. With the use of attributes and categories, products can be filtered and organized, simplifying the search process for customers. By following the steps provided in this article, you can proficiently manage your shop by brand in Magento 2, resulting in a more streamlined and customer-friendly online store.
