A Comprehensive Guide for Test Magento 2 Extensions on Mobile Devices

Analyze the popularity of mobile device statistics

The next step is to analyze the most in-demand devices in the countries you’ve identified. This will streamline the list of necessary devices for testing, leading to the elimination of some devices and prioritizing others.

There are numerous resources available for obtaining information on device popularity across different countries. Some of these include:

  •  appbrain.com, which provides statistics on Android device popularity worldwide
  •  deviceatlas.com, which offers information on the top 12 devices in various countries, including both iOS and Android devices
  •  gs.statcounter.com, which allows you to track the distribution of manufacturers across different countries over time, and provides customizable graphs based on your needs
  •  mixpanel.com, which provides statistics on Apple devices, including data on specific versions of iOS compared to previous versions and market trends for different iPhone models.

Check the prevalence of OS

Finally, consider the popularity of different operating system versions. By this point, you should already have a preliminary list of devices, which can be used to determine the most relevant operating systems. Some operating systems may even be excluded from consideration.

To determine the necessary operating systems, consider the following sources:

  •  developer.android.com, the official Google site that lists detailed versions of Android
  •  developer.apple.com, the official Apple site that displays currently popular versions of iOS
  •  appbrain.com, a service that allows you to evaluate the popularity of Android operating systems
  •  David-smith.org, a site that provides the distribution of older iOS versions by the device.

Points to remember

Having a comprehensive understanding of the market you operate in is crucial. It’s important to use the latest statistics, as data that’s more than a year old may not be applicable anymore, especially when it comes to mobile devices and operating systems. Steer clear of complicated lists that rank the most expensive, powerful, and popular devices, as these might not be relevant to your target market. For instance, the Samsung S10 may be a widely known and robust smartphone, but it may not be well-liked by users in India.

In every new extension, make sure to thoroughly check the display of all elements on mobile devices. Here is a list of basic checks to perform:

  •  Check the overall appearance and design.
  •  Test the functionality of each element in all states.
  •  Ensure all elements are clickable and their behavior is as expected.
  •  Check for compatibility with popular Magento 2 mobile themes.
  •  Verify that elements do not overlap.
  •  Test the scalability of an element.
  •  Check the functionality of links.
  •  Verify the proper functioning of any validation forms.
  •  Compare the display of elements in different browsers.
  •  Check if the letters match the declared design if the module has this feature.
