How to Unhide All Product Images on Product Page in Magento 2

Published on: February 01, 2023

Magento 2 is a highly flexible e-commerce platform that provides a wide range of customization options for website owners, including configurable products in Magento 2. One such customization option is the ability to hide or unhide product images on the product page. If you have accidentally hidden product images and want to make them visible again, this guide will walk you through the process of how to Unhide all Product Images on Product Page in Magento 2.

Steps to Unhide All Product Images on Product Page in Magento 2

Step 1: Log into Magento 2 Admin Panel

The first step is to log into the Magento 2 admin panel using your username and password.

Step 2: Navigate to the Product Grid

Once logged in, go to the “Products” section in the Magento 2 admin panel and click on the “Catalog” tab. This will take you to the product grid, where you can see a list of all your products.

Step 3: Select the Product

From the product grid, select the product whose images you want to unhide. You can do this by clicking on the checkbox next to the product name.

Step 4: Edit the Product

Once the product is selected, click on the “Edit” button at the top of the screen. This will take you to the product edit page.

Step 5: Navigate to the Images Tab

On the product edit page, navigate to the “Images” tab. This is where you can manage the images for the product.

Step 6: Unhide the Images

In the images tab, you will see a list of all the images for the product. Find the image that you want to unhide and click on the “Exclude” button. This will unhide the image and make it visible on the product page.

Step 7: Save Changes

Once you have unhidden all the necessary images, click on the “Save” button at the top of the screen to save the changes.

And that’s it! These simple steps will help you unhide all the product images on your product page in Magento 2.

Note: If you want to unhide all images at once, you can select all the images by clicking on the checkbox in the header row, and then click on the “Exclude” button.

Conclusion

In conclusion, Magento 2 provides a simple and straightforward way to unhide product images on the product page. By following the steps outlined in this guide, you can easily manage and display all the images for your products on your website. Whether you have accidentally hidden a single image or multiple images, this guide will help you unhide them and ensure that your customers can see all the necessary information and images for your products.
