How to Remove Billing and Shipping Address from Magento 2 Checkout – A Guide

In Magento 2 Checkout by Default: Billing and Shipping Address

Magento’s default checkout process consists of 3 sections: Billing & Shipping Address, Shipping Methods, and Payment Method, all of which are essential for a seamless shopping and selling experience.

In Magento 2, the Payment Method section typically displays the Billing Address, but it is only shown if the customer does not select the ‘My billing and shipping address are the same’ option. However, some store owners may want to customize the checkout process by relocating the Billing Address to the Shipping section and removing the Billing and Shipping Address checkboxes. If you are seeking guidance on how to accomplish this modification, we provide step-by-step instructions below on how to Remove Billing and shipping address from Checkout Magento 2.
