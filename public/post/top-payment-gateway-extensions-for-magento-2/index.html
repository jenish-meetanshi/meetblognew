Top Payment Gateway Extensions for Magento 2

Published on: August 29, 2024

Accepting payments online is essential for any e-commerce business. While Magento 2 provides built-in payment methods, using payment gateway extensions can enhance your store’s functionality and improve customer experience. In this article, we will explore the top 5 payment gateway extensions for Magento 2, each from a different company, to help you choose the best option for your store.

1. Magento 2 Alipay Payment

Company: Meetanshi
Magento 2 Alipay Cross Border Payment by Meetanshi helps your Magento 2 stores accept secure online payments using the Alipay Payment Gateway.

Key Features:

  • Accept payments from users in China and Hong Kong
  • Supports 16 currencies
  • Multi-store and multi-currency support

Alipay is a leading mobile and online payment platform by Alibaba Group. In China, it is the primary payment method for online transactions. This extension allows store owners to integrate Alipay into their Magento stores, making it easier to serve customers in China and Hong Kong. By providing the Alipay payment option, you can gain the trust of Chinese customers and facilitate smooth business transactions in these regions.

2. Magento 2 Stripe Payment Gateway

Company: Mageplaza
The Magento 2 Stripe Payment Gateway extension by Mageplaza enables you to integrate Stripe with your Magento store.

Key Features:

  • Secure payments with end-to-end encryption
  • Support for multiple currencies
  • Seamless checkout experience

Stripe is a widely recognized payment provider known for its security and ease of use. By integrating Stripe into your Magento 2 store, you can offer your customers a fast and reliable payment option, enhancing their shopping experience and boosting your sales.

3. Magento 2 Payeezy Payment Gateway

Company: Magenest
The Magento 2 Payeezy Payment Gateway extension allows you to integrate Payeezy, a payment processing solution by First Data, into your Magento store.

Key Features:

  • Supports credit card payments (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover)
  • Secure payment processing with PCI DSS compliance
  • Detailed transaction reports
  • Easy configuration and management

Payeezy is a reliable payment gateway that offers a secure and efficient way to process credit card payments for your Magento 2 store. By using this extension, you can provide your customers with a trusted payment option, improving their shopping experience and increasing your store’s conversion rates.

4. Magento 2 Benefit Payment Gateway

Company: Code Decorator
The Magento 2 Benefit Payment Gateway extension allows merchants to integrate Benefit, a payment processing solution, into their Magento store.

Key Features:

  • Supports multiple payment methods, including credit and debit cards
  • Secure payment transactions with advanced encryption
  • Easy setup and configuration
  • Comprehensive transaction management

Benefit Payment Gateway is designed to provide a seamless and secure payment experience for customers. By using this extension, you can enhance your Magento 2 store’s payment capabilities, ensuring that your customers enjoy a smooth checkout process.

5. Magento 2 Stripe Payment Gateway

Company: Rootways
The Magento 2 Stripe Payment Gateway extension by Rootways allows you to integrate Stripe, a popular payment processing solution, into your Magento store.

Key Features:

  • Choose appropriate payment action: Authorize Only or Authorize and Capture
  • Full or partial refund from website
  • Allow to cancel and void order transaction
  • Address verification
  • CVC verify or not, choose from admin
  • Order from admin
  • Support multiple currencies and multi-store
  • Safe and secure payment method

The Rootways Stripe Payment extension is easy to configure and install. You need to activate the payment method and add your Stripe information, and the Stripe Payment Method will be ready to use for your website.

Conclusion

Choosing the right payment gateway extension for your Magento 2 store can significantly enhance your customer’s shopping experience and streamline your payment processes. The five extensions mentioned above—Magento 2 Alipay Payment, Stripe, PayPal, Amazon Pay, and Braintree—each offer unique features that cater to different customer needs. Evaluate your business requirements and select the extension that best fits your goals to start accepting payments online with ease.
