What is the Tier Price in Magento

Published on: January 13, 2023

Competing in today’s cut-throat e-commerce market requires a well-thought-out pricing strategy, which can make or break a business. With this goal in mind, Magento offers a powerful feature known as “tier pricing” to equip merchants with the necessary tools to succeed.

Tier pricing allows merchants to set different prices for different quantities of a product, making it easier to attract bulk purchasers. This blog post will explore What is the Tier Price in Magento and how it works, and its benefits for your online store, such as increased sales, attracting new customers, and boosting your profitability.

What is Tier Pricing?

Tier pricing is a pricing strategy that offers customers a lower price for buying a higher quantity of a product. For example, a merchant may offer a lower price per unit for customers who purchase 10 or more of a particular product. This type of pricing strategy is often used to encourage bulk purchasing and increase sales.

How Does Tier Pricing Work in Magento?

In Magento, tier pricing is set up on a per-product basis. When creating or editing a product, merchants can access the “Tier Price” tab in the “Advanced Pricing” section. Here, they can set the quantity and price for each tier. For example, a merchant may set a tier price of $5 per unit for quantities between 1-9, and $4 per unit for quantities of 10 or more.

Once the tier pricing is set up, customers will see the different prices based on the quantity they select. For example, if a customer adds 10 units of a product to their cart, they will see the lower tier price of $4 per unit.

Benefits of Using Tier Pricing in Magento:

Encourages Bulk Purchasing: By offering a lower price for higher quantities, tier pricing encourages customers to buy more units of a product. This can lead to increased sales and revenue for the business.

Increases AOV: Average Order Value (AOV) is a metric that measures the average amount spent per order. By encouraging customers to purchase more units of a product, tier pricing can increase AOV and improve overall sales.

Create a sense of urgency: By using Tier pricing it can create a sense of urgency in customers to make a purchase before the price goes up.
